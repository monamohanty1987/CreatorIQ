## 1. SECTOR OVERVIEW

### What Is the Content Creator Economy?
The Creator Economy refers to the ecosystem of independent content creators --
individuals who build audiences on platforms (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok,
X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Substack) and monetise through brand partnerships,
digital products, affiliate marketing, subscriptions, and live events.

## 3. CORE PAIN POINTS (Research-Validated)

### Pain Point 1: Revenue Underoptimisation
- 72% of creators price digital products by gut feel (Gumroad Survey 2023)
- Average under-pricing gap: 35-50% below comparable market products
- Impact: Mid-tier creator leaves $8,400-$20,000/year on the table

### Pain Point 2: Algorithm Blindness
- 68% of creators experienced significant reach loss in the past 12 months
- Average time to detect: 6-7 weeks after the decline begins
- By detection time: 30-60% of reach already lost
- Impact: $6,400-$12,800/year in lost brand deal and ad revenue

### Pain Point 3: Brand Deal Exploitation
- Creators are routinely offered 40-70% below market rate
- 84% of creators have no data to validate or counter an offer
- 1 in 3 creators has unknowingly signed an unfair exclusivity clause
- FTC violation risk: $10,000-$50,000 first offense

### Pain Point 4: Content-Revenue Disconnect
- 85% of creators cannot identify which content piece generated a specific sale
- Average creator misallocates 40-60% of production time to low-converting formats
- Impact: 20-35% revenue increase possible from reallocation alone
